Our Story

Modern Day Warrior started as a desire for a gym that offered more than commercially manufactured fit lifestyles. We realized activities like dodgeball, sword fighting, and archery could provide full-body workouts and wanted to expand those options to be inclusive of all body types. Through communication and collaboration we built a gym that we would love and be proud of.
